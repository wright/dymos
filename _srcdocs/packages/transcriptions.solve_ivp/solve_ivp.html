class dymos.transcriptions.solve_ivp.solve_ivp.SolveIVP(grid_data=None, **kwargs)[source]¶

Bases: dymos.transcriptions.transcription_base.TranscriptionBase

The SolveIVP Transcription class.

SolveIVP transcription in Dymos uses the scipy.simulate.solve_ivp method to explicitly integrate the states from the phase initial time to the phase final time.

SolveIVP transcription does not currently support optimization since it does not propagate analytic derivatives through the ODE.
